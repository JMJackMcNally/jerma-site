Bio
Jeremy Elbertson, also known as Jerma985, is a former gaming YouTuber, turned Twitch Streamer, and is constantly innovating within the medium.
Also known as The Sus Guy, Jeremy has had the most mainstream notoriety with his famous Dollhouse Stream, which integrated theatre production to create a real life version of The Sims, with the audience influencing the show.
